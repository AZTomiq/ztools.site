üìö Corporate Income Tax Guide

1. What is Corporate Income Tax (CIT)?

Corporate Income Tax (CIT) is a direct tax levied on the taxable income of organizations and enterprises from production, trading, and other activities.

2. Who must pay CIT?

  • Enterprises established under Vietnamese law
  • Foreign enterprises with permanent establishments in Vietnam
  • Cooperatives and other organizations
  • Other organizations with business activities

3. Calculation Steps

Step 1: Calculate Taxable Income

Taxable Income = Revenue - Deductible Expenses + Other Income

Step 2: Calculate Income for Tax

Income for Tax = Taxable Income - Exempt Income - Losses Carried Forward

4. Deadlines

  • Annual Settlement: Last day of the 3rd month after financial year-end
  • Quarterly Provisional: Must pay 75% of total tax liability by Q3 deadline
  • Payment: Same deadline as tax declaration

5. Tax Exemptions

  • Income from agriculture, forestry, fishery
  • Income from scientific research and technology development
  • Income from education, healthcare, culture, charity
  • Income from capital contribution, share purchase (taxed previously)
  • Income from vocational training for disabilities/minorities
