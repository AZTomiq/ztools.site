Text Diff Checker - How to Use

  1. Paste Original Text: Copy and paste the original version of your text into the left (or first) box.
  2. Paste Modified Text: Copy and paste the new version of your text into the right (or second) box.
  3. Click Compare: Hit the "Compare Text" button.
  4. View Results:
    • Side-by-Side: Useful for seeing the structure changes. Deletions are red (left), Insertions are green (right).
    • Inline: Useful for reading the flow. Deletions are crossed out in red, insertions are underlined in green.

Tips:

  • Use "Clear" to reset both fields.
  • This tool runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server.
