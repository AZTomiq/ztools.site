### How to generate strong passwords

1.  **Length**: Adjust the slider to choose password length (up to 50 chars). Better length = better security.
2.  **Options**:
    *   **ABC**: Include uppercase letters.
    *   **abc**: Include lowercase letters.
    *   **123**: Include numbers.
    *   **!@#**: Include special symbols ($ @ % & ...).
3.  **Generate**: Click the refresh icon to generate a new random password.
4.  **Copy**: Click the copy icon to save it to your clipboard.

**Security Note:**
All passwords are generated locally in your browser. Nothing is ever sent to our servers.
