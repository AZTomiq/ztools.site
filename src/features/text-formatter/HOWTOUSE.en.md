### How to use Text Formatter

1.  **Input**: Paste your raw text into the input field.
2.  **Select Options**:
    *   **Remove extra spaces**: Cleans up multiple spaces between words.
    *   **Remove empty lines**: Deletes blank lines from the text.
    *   **Fix punctuation**: Corrects spacing around periods, commas, etc.
    *   **Case conversion**: Convert to UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, etc.
3.  **Copy**: Click "Copy" to get your polished text.
