### How to calculate your BMI

1.  **Enter Details**:
    *   **Gender**: Select Male or Female.
    *   **Age**: Enter your age.
    *   **Height**: Enter your height in centimeters (cm).
    *   **Weight**: Enter your weight in kilograms (kg).
2.  **Results**:
    *   **BMI Score**: Your Body Mass Index.
    *   **Classification**: Underweight, Normal, Overweight, or Obese.
    *   **Ideal Weight**: The recommended weight range for your height.
