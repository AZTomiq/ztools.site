### How to use Word Counter

1.  **Input Text**: Type or paste your content into the main text area.
2.  **View Stats**:
    *   **Words**: Total word count.
    *   **Characters**: Total characters (including spaces).
    *   **Sentences**: Number of sentences detected.
    *   **Paragraphs**: Number of paragraphs detected.
3.  **Extra Features**:
    *   **Reading/Speaking Time**: Estimated time to read or speak the text.
    *   **Keyword Analysis**: See the most frequent words (keyword density).
