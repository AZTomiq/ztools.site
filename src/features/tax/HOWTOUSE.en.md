### How to use Personal Income Tax Calculator (Vietnam)

1.  **Income**: Enter your total monthly income (gross salary).
2.  **Insurance Salary**: Enter the salary amount used to calculate mandatory insurance contributions (Social, Health, Unemployment). Often this is the same as your Gross Income.
3.  **Dependents**: Enter the number of registered dependents (children, elderly parents). Each dependent provides a deduction of 4.4 million VND/month.
4.  **Region**: Select your region to determine the minimum wage cap for Unemployment Insurance.
    *   **Region I**: Major cities like Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City.
    *   **Region II, III, IV**: Other provinces and rural areas.
5.  **View Results**: The tool calculates your taxable income, tax amount, and your final Net Salary.

**Notes:**
*   Standard personal deduction: 11 million VND/month.
*   Dependent deduction: 4.4 million VND/person/month.
*   Insurance rates: Social (8%), Health (1.5%), Unemployment (1%).
